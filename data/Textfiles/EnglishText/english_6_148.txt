Those who give partners (to Allah) will say: &quot;If Allah had wished, we should not have given partners to Him nor would our fathers; nor should we have had any taboos.&quot; So did their ancestors argue falsely, until they tasted of Our wrath. Say: &quot;Have ye any (certain) knowledge? If so, produce it before us. Ye follow nothing but conjecture: ye do nothing but lie.&quot;
