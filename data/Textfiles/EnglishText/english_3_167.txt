And the Hypocrites also. These were told: &quot;Come, fight in the way of Allah, or (at least) drive (The foe from your city).&quot; They said: &quot;Had we known how to fight, we should certainly have followed you.&quot; They were that day nearer to Unbelief than to Faith, saying with their lips what was not in their hearts but Allah hath full knowledge of all they conceal.
