He will say: &quot;Enter ye in the company of the peoples who passed away before you - men and jinns, - into the Fire.&quot; Every time a new people enters, it curses its sister-people (that went before), until they follow each other, all into the Fire. Saith the last about the first: &quot;Our Lord! it is these that misled us: so give them a double penalty in the Fire.&quot; He will say: &quot;Doubled for all&quot; : but this ye do not understand.
