And when he arrived at the watering (place) in Madyan, he found there a group of men watering (their flocks), and besides them he found two women who were keeping back (their flocks). He said: &quot;What is the matter with you?&quot; They said: &quot;We cannot water (our flocks) until the shepherds take back (their flocks): And our father is a very old man.&quot;
