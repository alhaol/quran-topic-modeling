No just estimate of Allah do they make when they say: &quot;Nothing doth Allah send down to man (by way of revelation)&quot; Say: &quot;Who then sent down the Book which Moses brought?- a light and guidance to man: But ye make it into (separate) sheets for show, while ye conceal much (of its contents): therein were ye taught that which ye knew not- neither ye nor your fathers.&quot; Say: &quot;Allah (sent it down)&quot;: Then leave them to plunge in vain discourse and trifling.
