They ask thee about the (final) Hour - when will be its appointed time? Say: &quot;The knowledge thereof is with my Lord (alone): None but He can reveal as to when it will occur. Heavy were its burden through the heavens and the earth. Only, all of a sudden will it come to you.&quot; They ask thee as if thou Wert eager in search thereof: Say: &quot;The knowledge thereof is with Allah (alone), but most men know not.&quot;
