Among the People of the Book are some who, if entrusted with a hoard of gold, will (readily) pay it back; others, who, if entrusted with a single silver coin, will not repay it unless thou constantly stoodest demanding, because, they say, &quot;there is no call on us (to keep faith) with these ignorant (Pagans).&quot; but they tell a lie against Allah, and (well) they know it.
